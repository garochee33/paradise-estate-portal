## OCTOBER 2026 — Closing Season

### Market Context
- **Official Closing Season** rates apply until October 24, 2026
- Estate typically moves into winter maintenance mode after Oct 24
- Aegean sea still warm (~21°C), air cools (~19°C) — swimming still viable
- Major clubs (Cavo Paradiso, Scorpios) host closing parties first week of October
- **After October 10:** Marketing pivots entirely to Privacy, Wellness, and Photography
- Primary revenue source: European "weekend break" travelers

### Individual Suites (October)

| Suite | Rate | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| The Rock House | €200–€280/night | "Staycation" locals from Athens, weekend travelers |
| Cinderella Suite | €200–€280/night | Fraction of July price — luxury at value |

### October Booking Strategy
- **Lower minimum stays:** Shift to 2–3 night minimum
- **Heated Pool USP:** If pools are heated (Carina), this is the #1 headline. 28°C pool vs. 15°C evening air = booking vs. bounce.
- **Target Audiences:**
  - **Digital Nomads:** "Work from Paradise" 14-day package at 20% discount
  - **Micro-Weddings:** 20–30 people, private church, smaller budget than June clients
  - **Corporate Retreats:** €28,980/week full estate is highly competitive. Pitch European tech firms in September for October "Strategy Retreats" — amphitheatre as outdoor boardroom.

### October Operational Plan — Winterization Checklist
1. **Staffing Drawdown:** Reduce housekeeping and concierge by 50% by October 15. Move to "On-Call" for smaller suites.
2. **Outdoor Maintenance:** Salt air most corrosive in autumn. Deep-clean + protective coating for all outdoor marble tables and sound system components by October 25.
3. **Climate Resilience Fee:** Continue collecting €15/night. Disclose clearly — guests more price-sensitive.
4. **Inventory Audit:** Use lower occupancy mid-October for full "linen and glass" audit across all three villas. Prepare replacement orders for 2027 season.
