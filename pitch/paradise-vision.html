The Problem with Tickets

❌ Selling Tickets

  • Cheapens the brand instantly
  • Attracts randoms — no curation
  • Price ceiling: €100–€200/person max
  • One-off revenue, no relationship
  • Competes with every club on the island
  • Requires constant promotion each event
  • No exclusivity — anyone can buy in
  • Legal/licensing complexity for public events

✅ Members Club

  • Elevates the brand — access, not admission
  • Curated community of high-value people
  • No price ceiling: €1,500–€15,000/year
  • Recurring annual revenue
  • Nobody else on Mykonos has this
  • Members promote for you (referrals)
  • Exclusivity drives demand
  • Private event = no public licensing needed

The truth: High-level people don't buy tickets to villa parties. They get invited. They're on the list. They belong to something. A membership gives them that belonging — and gives you recurring revenue instead of one-night cash.

How the July Party Works (No Tickets)

  1. Announce the Paradise Estate Members Club — the July party is the inaugural event
  2. Founding member pricing — first 50 sign-ups get 30% off (locked for life)
  3. The party is "free" for members — it's their first benefit
  4. Non-members attend by invitation only — as guests of members, or by signing up at the door
  5. Revenue comes from: memberships + bottle service + bar packages + brand sponsors
