# Paradise Estate Mykonos — Master Plan

**Date:** 2026-05-04
**Prepared by:** Enzo Garoche
**Status:** DRAFT — Internal Planning Document
**Purpose:** Strategic plan for taking over vacation rental management and luxury experience curation at Paradise Estate Mykonos.

---

## Executive Summary

Paradise Estate Mykonos is a 10,000 m² luxury compound in Pirgi with estate-level amenities (helipad, private church, amphitheatre, professional kitchen) currently positioned as mid-luxury ($768–$2,835/night) despite having ultra-luxury infrastructure. The property suffers from dormant marketing (Instagram dead since 2023), a critical social proof deficit (3 total public reviews), inconsistent brand messaging, no direct booking engine, and significant untapped revenue streams (weddings, events, experiences).

This plan outlines a phased approach to take over rental management, fix the foundational problems, build a luxury experience program, and scale revenue — targeting a **40–60% revenue increase within 12 months** through rate optimization, experience monetization, direct bookings, and brand repositioning.

---

## PHASE 0: DEAL STRUCTURE & OWNER PROPOSAL (Weeks 1–2)

### 0.1 Proposed Management Model

**Option A — Revenue Share (Recommended)**
- 20–25% commission on all gross rental revenue
- 30–40% commission on all experience/service revenue (higher margin, we build it)
- Owner retains property ownership, insurance, major maintenance
- We handle: marketing, bookings, guest experience, operations, staffing coordination

**Option B — Fixed Management Fee + Performance Bonus**
- Fixed monthly retainer (€3,000–€5,000/month during season, €1,500 off-season)
- Plus 10% performance bonus on revenue exceeding agreed targets
- Lower risk for us, less upside

**Option C — Lease & Operate**
- Fixed annual lease payment to owner (negotiate based on historical revenue)
- We operate fully independently, keep all revenue above lease cost
- Highest risk, highest upside

### 0.2 What We Bring to the Table
1. Professional revenue management and dynamic pricing
2. Full digital marketing overhaul (website, social, SEO, paid ads)
3. Direct booking strategy to reduce OTA commission leakage
4. Luxury experience program design and execution
5. Wedding & event sales pipeline
6. Review generation and social proof building
7. Brand repositioning from mid-luxury to upper-luxury
8. Operational SOPs and quality standards
9. Guest CRM and retention program
10. Multi-language guest communications

### 0.3 What We Need from the Owner
1. Exclusive management rights (minimum 2-year term, ideally 3)
2. Access to all existing OTA accounts and booking platforms
3. Access to historical booking data and revenue figures
4. Authority to set pricing and minimum stays
5. Budget approval for initial investments (photography, website, marketing)
6. Introduction to existing staff and vendor relationships
7. Property access for inspection and content creation
8. Agreement on capital improvement priorities (if any)

### 0.4 Proposal Deliverables
- [ ] Professional pitch deck (10–15 slides)

## PHASE 2: BRAND REPOSITIONING (Weeks 4–8)

*Goal: Resolve the identity crisis and position as upper-luxury.*

### 2.1 Brand Strategy

**Current Problem:** The brand tries to be both "serene luxury retreat" AND "party destination" simultaneously. This confuses the market and caps pricing.

**New Positioning:** *"A versatile private estate for extraordinary moments"*

- Lead with: serenity, exclusivity, Cycladic elegance, panoramic views, privacy
- Secondary discovery: event capability, entertainment infrastructure, party-ready
- The estate adapts to the guest — not the other way around
